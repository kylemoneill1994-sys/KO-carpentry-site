Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How much does window replacement cost in Kansas City?

Window replacement with KO Carpentry typically starts around $900 per window, installed. The final price depends on window size, style, and any upgrades like triple-pane glass or custom frame colors. We provide a free, no-obligation in-home estimate so you know the exact cost before committing.

How much does a new entry or patio door cost?

Doors typically start around $2,500 installed, depending on the door style, material, and any glass or hardware upgrades. We install ProVia entry and patio doors and Alliance Hawthorne patio doors, both backed by strong manufacturer warranties.

How long does installation take?

Window replacement typically takes 2–4 weeks from order to install, while doors usually take 3–6 weeks depending on customization options chosen. Most installation appointments themselves are completed in a single day.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We offer financing through Acorn Finance. You can get pre-qualified online in seconds with no impact to your credit score, and choose a monthly payment plan that fits your budget.

What areas do you serve?

KO Carpentry proudly serves Kansas City and the entire surrounding metro area, including Overland Park, Lee's Summit, Blue Springs, Independence, Olathe, and all nearby suburbs on both the Missouri and Kansas sides.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, KO Carpentry LLC is fully licensed (Lic. # KO-2024-9821) and carries full liability insurance, so your home is protected throughout the entire installation process.

Why are your prices lower than the big window companies?

As a solo operator with 15+ years of experience — including past work for two of the major window and door companies in Kansas City — KO Carpentry has significantly lower overhead than large companies. That savings is passed directly to you without sacrificing quality, craftsmanship, or warranty coverage.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, every installation is backed by a lifetime workmanship warranty from KO Carpentry, in addition to the manufacturer warranties on the windows and doors themselves.
